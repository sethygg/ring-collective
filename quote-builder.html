Not sure of your size? Click here for help
Quick ways to find your ring size:

Option 1: Borrow a ring they already wear on that finger and bring it to any local jeweler for a free measurement.

Option 2: Wrap a thin strip of paper around the finger, mark where it overlaps, and measure the length in millimeters. Divide by 3.14 to get the diameter.

Option 3: Select "I'm not sure" and we'll help you figure it out!

💎 Why We Recommend Lab Diamonds

At The Ring Collective, we specialize in lab-grown diamonds — and here's why our customers love them:

  • Chemically, physically, and optically identical to mined diamonds
  • 40-60% less expensive — get a bigger, better stone for your budget
  • Ethically sourced with zero mining impact
  • Every stone is certified and graded to the same standards

The truth is, even a trained jeweler can't tell the difference without specialized equipment. You get the same fire, brilliance, and forever-quality — at a price that makes sense.

We don't source natural mined stones, but we're happy to create your perfect setting if you already have a stone or prefer to source your own.

💍 Setting Only — Great Choice!

We'll craft your custom ring setting to your exact specifications. Once you receive it, simply take it to any local jeweler to have your stone set. This is a popular option for customers who already have a family stone, want to source their own diamond, or prefer to shop for the perfect stone in person.
