Our Story

Why We Started The Ring Collective

For the last decade, we've been the jewelers behind the jewelers — crafting custom pieces for retail stores across the country. Thirty-plus years of combined craftsmanship sat behind every ring that left our workshop, only to be marked up 2× or more by the time it reached a display case.

We saw the receipts. And we knew it didn't have to be that way.

The Ring Collective is our answer: the exact same hands, the exact same quality, at the exact same price the jewelry store pays. No storefront. No markup. Just couples, straight to the bench.
